The idea

Planning apps make you do the planning.

Every to-do app hands you an empty grid and makes you figure out when to do everything, drag blocks around, and remember to leave on time. People don't think in calendars — they think in quick fragments: a thought on the walk, a reminder fired off mid-meeting.

Planfect's bet: you should be able to say one casual sentence — even a whole afternoon at once, by voice — and get a realistic, scheduled day back in seconds. The app does the when: it fits things around your work and sleep, adds real travel time so you arrive on time, learns your habits, and only asks when it's genuinely unsure.

想法

市面上的日程 app,都得你自己来规划。

每个待办 app 都丢给你一个空白格子,让你自己想什么时候做、拖来拖去、还得记着几点出门。可人不是按日历思考的——人是冒出碎片想法:走路时想起一件事、开会间隙顺手记一笔。

Planfect 的核心赌注:你只要随口说一句——甚至语音一口气说一下午的安排——几秒钟就能换回排好的一天。"什么时候做"交给 app:它绕开你的工作和睡觉时间、算上真实路程让你准点到、学你的习惯,只有真的拿不准才问你一句。

How it's built

Architecture

A native iOS client, a serverless TypeScript backend, and an LLM agent that doesn't just chat — it reasons about your schedule, calls tools, and commits real database writes.

怎么做的

技术架构

一个原生 iOS 客户端、一个 Serverless 的 TypeScript 后端,加上一个会"干活"的 LLM agent——它不只是聊天,而是真的推理你的日程、调用工具、并写进数据库。

iOS · SwiftUI

Chat UI w/ markdown, day/week/month timetable, Swift Charts insights, WidgetKit (App Groups), EventKit, Speech, Sign in with Apple, notifications.

Backend · Supabase

Postgres + RLS, Edge Functions in Deno/TypeScript, auth, migrations. A timezone-aware scheduling engine (pure, unit-tested) places tasks into free slots.

AI · agent loop

Multi-step tool-calling over OpenAI: reads context, proposes, confirms, then writes. Pluggable provider/model; usage & cost logged.

iOS · SwiftUI

带 Markdown 的聊天界面、日/周/月时间表、Swift Charts 分析图、WidgetKit 小组件(App Groups)、EventKit、语音识别、Apple 登录、本地通知。

后端 · Supabase

Postgres + 行级安全(RLS)、Deno/TypeScript 的 Edge Functions、鉴权、数据库迁移。一个时区感知的排程引擎(纯函数、有单元测试)把任务放进空闲时段。

AI · agent 循环

在 OpenAI 上做多步工具调用:读上下文 → 提议 → 确认 → 写库。模型/服务商可插拔;用量与成本都有记录。

Engineering highlights

The hard parts

工程亮点

难点都在哪

🌍 Timezone-correct scheduling

The planner schedules in the user's local time, and looks up cross-timezone events (a London match watched from the US) and converts to their clock. Fixed a class of bugs where “today” drifted to a past date in long, multi-day chats — by stamping the authoritative date onto the latest turn for recency.

🧭 Product-minded placement

Commute is scheduled before a pinned task (you leave early, arrive on time — not the activity pushed later). A voice brain-dump of several tasks is planned in one batch; genuine conflicts are surfaced, never silently mis-scheduled. When a detail is still undecided — which restaurant, exactly where — it blocks a tentative hold you can refine in a tap, instead of interrogating you or scheduling nothing.

⚡ Latency, halved

Cut planner latency ~60% by short-circuiting a redundant LLM round-trip once tasks land on the calendar, skipping needless schedule lookups (the calendar is already in context), and parsing dates once at decode.

🔒 Shipping rigor

RLS-secured data, App Groups for the widget, distribution signing → TestFlight → App Store pipeline (automated via API key), a password-gated analytics dashboard, CI, and prompt-level content guardrails.

🗓 Calendar sync that never touches your stuff

Plans sync into a dedicated “Planfect” calendar, reconciled — create / move / delete — against a planfect://block/<id> marker stamped on each event. Edits and deletions in the app flow to Apple Calendar, while your personal events are never read or modified; the planner also excludes its own calendar when reading “busy,” so it never plans around itself.

🌐 Instant in-app language switch

Flipping 中文 ⇄ EN in Settings re-skins the whole app immediately — no relaunch. A Bundle.main reclass redirects every localized lookup to the chosen .lproj at runtime, and the view tree re-renders keyed on the language while the one-time startup work stays put.

🌍 时区正确的排程

排程一律按用户本地时间;查跨时区赛事(在美国看伦敦的比赛)会自动换算成你的钟点。还修掉了一类 bug:跨好几天的长对话里"今天"会漂到过去的日期——办法是把权威日期戳到最新一条消息上,靠"就近"压住旧日期。

🧭 有产品思维的排法

通勤排在定点任务之前(你提前出门、准点到,而不是把活动往后推)。语音一口气报好几件事会被一次性批量排好;真正的冲突会提示你,绝不偷偷排错。细节还没定(哪家店、具体在哪)时,会先占一个临时位,之后一点就能改,而不是反问你或干脆什么都不排。

⚡ 延迟砍掉一半

排程延迟降了约 60%:任务一落到日历就短路掉一次多余的 LLM 来回、省掉没必要的日程查询(日历本来就在上下文里)、日期只在解码时解析一次。

🔒 上线工程的严谨度

RLS 保护的数据、小组件用的 App Groups、发布签名 → TestFlight → App Store 全流程(用 API key 自动化)、带密码保护的分析后台、CI、以及提示词层面的内容护栏。

🗓 只动自己那本的日历同步

计划会同步进一本专属的「Planfect」日历,按写在每个事件上的 planfect://block/<id> 标记做对账——新建 / 移动 / 删除。你在 app 里改或删,都会同步到 Apple 日历,而你自己的私人事件从不被读取或改动;规划器读「忙碌」时也会排除自己这本日历,绝不围着自己排程。

🌐 即时切换 App 语言

在设置里 中文 ⇄ EN 一切,整个 app 立刻换皮、不用重启。靠把 Bundle.main 重新归类、运行时把每次本地化查找重定向到选定的 .lproj,再让视图树按语言重渲染,而一次性的启动逻辑原地不动。
