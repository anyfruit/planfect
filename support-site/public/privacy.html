隐私政策

最后更新:2026 年 6 月

Planfect(“我们”)是一款 AI 日程助手。我们只收集让 App 正常工作所必需的数据,绝不出售你的个人信息。

我们收集的信息

  • 账户信息:你注册时提供的电子邮箱(用于登录)。
  • 你创建的内容:你的日程、任务、作息、偏好,以及你为估算通勤而输入的地址。

我们如何使用这些信息

  • 用于为你生成和管理日程安排(App 的核心功能)。
  • 为了生成日程,你输入的内容(聊天消息,以及为安排它们所需的任务、作息和日程信息)会发送给第三方 AI 服务商进行处理——国际版为 OpenAI,中国大陆为 MiniMax;你输入的地址会发送给 Google Maps 估算通勤时间。这些服务商仅按我们的指示处理你的数据、不得另作他用,并提供与本政策同等的数据保护。我们会在 App 内首次使用 AI 规划前明确告知并征得你的同意。
  • 数据存储在我们的后端服务商 Supabase 上。
  • 我们不会把你的数据用于广告或跨 App 跟踪,也不会出售给第三方。

Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

Planfect ("we") is an AI day-planning assistant. We collect only the data needed to make the app work, and we never sell your personal information.

Information we collect

  • Account info: the email address you sign up with (for login).
  • Content you create: your plans, tasks, routine, preferences, and any addresses you enter for travel-time estimates.

How we use it

  • To build and manage your schedule (the app's core function).
  • To generate your schedule, the content you type (your chat messages, plus the task, routine, and schedule details needed to plan them) is sent to a third-party AI provider for processing — OpenAI internationally, or MiniMax for users in mainland China; addresses you enter are sent to Google Maps for travel times. These providers process your data only on our instructions, not for their own purposes, and provide protection equivalent to this policy. We disclose this and ask for your consent in the app before you first use AI planning.
  • Data is stored with our backend provider, Supabase.
  • We do not use your data for advertising or cross-app tracking, and we do not sell it to third parties.
