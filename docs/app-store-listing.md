## App Privacy (your declaration — verify before submitting)

Planfect collects, **linked to the user's identity**, for **App Functionality** (not tracking):
- **Contact Info → Email address** (account sign-in).
- **User Content** (the plans/tasks/notes you create and your routine).
- (If you keep it) **Coarse/precise Location or addresses** you enter for travel-time estimates.

Data is sent to service providers to make the app work: task text to the LLM provider (OpenAI) for
planning, and addresses to Google Maps for travel times. **Tracking: No.** Confirm these answers match
reality in App Store Connect → App Privacy before submitting.
